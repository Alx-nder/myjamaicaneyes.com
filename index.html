Through My Jamaican Eyes Book 1

MY AMERICAN REALITY

Many came from far and wide for a chance to live the American Dream. For some, the American Dream was alive and well. Others were just barely alive, hoping for a chance at the American Dream. Many succumbed to the beatings and were forced to accept the harshness of the American Reality. Some elected to take control of their own destiny by creating an Alternate American Reality. This book, inspired by actual events, sheds some light into the darkness, thus bringing to vision, the ugliness, illusion, delusion, and confusion, lining the perilous path between the American Dream, and the American Reality.

Through My Jamaican Eyes Book 2

AMERICA: I Am Race

We spend so much time thinking of ourselves as race and gender, that we lose sight as to who we are as humans. Race, as a human construct, is so deeply embedded into the fabric of our social consciousness, that when stretched and pulled by opposing ideas, the sound of the ripping is indisputable; the tear is unrepairable. The morphing of this invention into our fragile state of race, has had a profound effect on our civility. This tug-of-war has scrambled AMERICA, to serve us a putrid dish of I AM RACE. The stories, poems and quotes contained in this bock were inspired by actual events depicting the manifestation of current race-based society.
